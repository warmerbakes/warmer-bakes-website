Who We Are

We are a small home kitchen that loves to cook and bake. Everything we make comes from old family recipes passed down through generations.

We make cakes, pies, pickles, and real East Indian food like Bottle Masala and Vindaloo — all made fresh, just for you.

We only make your order when you place it — no stock, no preservatives, no shortcuts. Just good, honest food made with care.
